## ğŸ›¡ï¸ PMR446 Compliance

The Midland G18 operates on **PMR446** (Private Mobile Radio) frequencies, which are:

- **License-free** in most European countries
- **Power limited** to 0.5W ERP (Effective Radiated Power)
- **Frequency range**: 446.00625 - 446.19375 MHz
- **Channel bandwidth**: 12.5 kHz

### Regional Considerations
- **Europe**: PMR446 channels are standardized and license-free
- **Switzerland**: Specific regulations may apply - consult local authorities
- **Other regions**: Verify local frequency allocations and licensing requirements

### Compliance Best Practices
- Use only approved frequencies for your region
- Respect power limitations
- Follow local emergency channel protocols
- Maintain equipment within certification standards
