## ‚öôÔ∏è Complete Configuration Guide

### üîã General Settings

#### 1. TX Power Management
- **Default Setting**: **Low Power** (0.5W)
  - **Benefit**: Extends battery life significantly
  - **Range**: 1-3 km in open terrain
  - **Best For**: Close-range communication, battery conservation

- **High Power Toggle**: **PF4 Hold** button
  - **Setting**: **High Power** (5W)
  - **Benefit**: Maximum range when needed
  - **Range**: 3-8 km in optimal conditions
  - **Best For**: Long-range communication, emergency situations

#### 2. Bandwidth Configuration
- **Setting**: **Narrow (12.5 kHz)**
  - **Benefit**: Clearer audio quality
  - **Compliance**: Meets modern regulatory standards
  - **Interference**: Reduced susceptibility to adjacent channels

#### 3. Busy Channel Lockout (BCL)
- **Setting**: **Enabled**
  - **Purpose**: Prevents transmission on occupied channels
  - **Benefit**: Avoids interference with existing communications
  - **Etiquette**: Promotes proper radio communication protocols

#### 4. Time-Out Timer (TOT)
- **Setting**: **3 minutes**
  - **Purpose**: Prevents accidental extended transmissions
  - **Benefit**: Conserves battery and promotes channel sharing
  - **Safety**: Prevents radio lock-up situations
