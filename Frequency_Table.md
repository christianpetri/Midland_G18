### Advanced Configuration Options

#### CTCSS (Continuous Tone-Coded Squelch System)
- **Purpose**: Reduces interference from other users
- **Recommendation**: Use on busy channels (1, 3, 7)
- **Tone Selection**: Choose from standard CTCSS tones
- **Coordination**: Ensure all group members use same tone

#### Power Management
- **Low Power**: 0.5W - Extends battery life, suitable for short range
- **High Power**: 5W - Maximum range, higher battery consumption
- **Auto Selection**: Program PF4 Hold for power toggle

#### Channel Features
- **Scan**: Program to monitor multiple channels
- **Monitor**: Enable for weak signal detection
- **BCL**: Busy Channel Lockout prevents transmission interference
